– Who are we? –

Evento Agency was founded in 2019. after a long time of following fashion world. High fashion brands were in the center of attention. We followed their work and the way they represented collections on runways. Since making a high quality pieces for new seasons' collection was always a very hard and stressful work, we wanted to take the responsibility of organizing the whole fashion show to make designers feel less overwhelmed when the time for show or fashion weeks come. Nowadays, we are the first number designers call when they start working on a new season.
