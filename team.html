Meet our wonderful team

It was not easy for us to find high quality professionals, but when we found each other, all the pieces came together. We have been friends and colleagues for a couple of years before this agency was formed and later on we realized that we all have passion for the same thing - fashion, so we decided to start a business together. That was a completely new step into fashion industry and a huge risk, but we were all determined to step into it. We give our best in every single project and we cannot wait for the upcoming ones!

Maria

The head of fashion shows interior. She always combine it with the theme of a collection and what it is inspired by. Full of imagination, her work is always unique because she gives her soul in every single project.

Karen

Whenever a big gala event is getting closer, she is the center of attention. She organizes everything - from the list of guests till the food menu. Her name is behind all the big world-famous events. There is no end to her imagination.

Petar

The right hand of Maria and Karen. His responsibility is to arrage everything they organize - exact time of a show/event, place of a show/event, whether it is a building or public place and he provides all the necessary documentation.

Alma

The mastermind of a company finances. She is a head of Finance and Accounting section and takes care of everything related to incomes and outcomes indoor agency and she does her job very diligently.

Paul

Our main photographer. An indescribable talent and sharp eyesight for details. He is full of experience and did a lot of covers and campaigns with many world-famous fashion brands and models.

Siena

Since she is the most comunicative person we have ever met, is it not strange that she is the head of a Commercial. Her job is to sign contracts with our customers and to convey their wishes to us.
