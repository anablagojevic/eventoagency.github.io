⋅ Short story about us ⋅

Founded in 2019. Our agency menaged to achieve a large number of clients very quickly. At the beginning, we only organized fashion shows, but later on, we started doing big gala events too. We quickly gained the trust of our client, and since we have started, we have not lost a single client. Our agency is behind almost every world-famous event, including met gala, whitch is the most important event in the world of fashion industry. Our team is very hard-working and we are always looking forward to new project. Down below you can see a couple of project that we have done.

What they are

The Met Gala, – known as the Met Ball, is an annual fundraising gala for the benefit of the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Costume Institute in New York City.
Paris Fashion Week – is a series of designer presentations held semiannually in Paris, France with spring/summer and autumn/winter events held each year.
Balmain – is a French luxury fashion house that was founded by Pierre Balmain in 1945. It currently operates 16 monobrand stores, including locations in New York City, London, Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Miami, and in Milan's Via Montenapoleone.
Karl Otto Lagerfeld – was a German creative director, fashion designer, artist, photographer, and caricaturist. He was known as the creative director of Chanel, Fendi, and of his own eponymous fashion label.
